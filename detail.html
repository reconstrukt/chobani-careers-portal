Chobani’s Commercial Finance Team drives effective and efficient customer investment by partnering with the business and supporting them in the commercial decision-making process. The Finance Analyst for the Commercial group will work in partnership with business stakeholders to provide actionable analysis and recommendations to senior leadership and functional partners. This role will support forecasting, planning, and reporting processes that drive our commercial growth strategies.   

The responsibilities of this position include:

  • Conduct in-depth analysis of topline drivers across both customers and products in order to support delivery of financial targets
  • Synthesize pricing and trade data to draw insights and recommend action plans to find efficiencies and drive profitable growth
  • Partner closely with sales and control to ensure accurate trade accruals and help manage quarterly and annual audit review processes
  • Lead and own data integrity in new cloud-based financial forecasting and planning tool
  • Create and maintain consistent and actionable scorecard reporting
  • Serve as liaison between various business partners to ensure alignment during planning and forecasting processes as well as reporting
  • Participate in ad-hoc projects to improve reporting, analytical tools and internal processes to enhance management decision process

The requirements of this position include:

  • Bachelor’s in Finance, Accounting, or equivalent degree preferred
  • 1-2 years of experience in CPG or other relevant finance experience
  • Basic MS Excel/Power Point skills required
  • Highly organized and attentive to detail, with the ability to work quickly and accurately and to consistently meet deadlines
  • Willingness to learn and ability to adapt and balance competing priorities in a fast-paced work environment
  • Proactive nature, with demonstrated ability to take initiative in problem-solving situations
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills
  • Strong interpersonal skills, with the ability to work collaboratively and on cross-functional teams
