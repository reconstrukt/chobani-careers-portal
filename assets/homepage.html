Chobani perks

Paid parental leave

Our paid parental leave policy provides our employees who have completed at least six months of continuous full-time employment with six weeks of 100% gender equitable paid leave to care for a child following a birth, adoption, or foster care placement, regardless of their level. We want our employees to be able to give the newest additions to their families the care they so need and return to their jobs feeling confident and ready.

Chobani gives

“Giving Back” is in our DNA. We take great pride in how much time our employees give not only to our Chobani-led engagements, but also to their own charitable causes. We multiply that giving back power by offering each employee eight paid hours of volunteer time every year – “Chobani Gives” – to be used when they roll up their sleeves at the organization or for the cause of their choice.

Inclusion and Diversity

We want everyone to feel like they can be their authentic selves in the workplace and are committed to creating a diverse and inclusive environment. Employee resource groups provide a safe place to build community and increase cultural competency. From 2019-2022, Chobani was recognized as “Best Places to Work for LGBTQ Equality” and has received a perfect 100 on the Human Rights Campaign’s Corporate Equality Index.

Comprehensive benefits

In addition to paid parental leave, we offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, vision coverage, 401k match, short and long-term disability coverage, health savings accounts, flexible spending accounts, and tuition reimbursement. We’re also proud to offer specialized benefits like health care navigation, mental health services, fertility assistance, gender reassignment services and more.

Community engagement

Volunteering time is just one way we give back to the places we call home. Throughout the year, we also support our communities through scholarships, impact grants and event sponsorships. And across the country, we donate millions of products every year to schools, food pantries, and other non-profit organizations partnering with us to combat food insecurity and provide access to healthier food choices.

Wellness resources

Included in our mission of better food for more people are the wellness and happiness of our employees and their families, and as such, we are pleased to provide them with wellness resources including fitness brand discounts as well as gym membership reimbursements, an employee assistance program, a health advocate program, and easy access to Chobani products and other assistance programs, including an onsite café and all-access gym in Idaho.

Chobani Employee Spotlight

Chobani values

Craftsmanship

Every day, we make wholesome products by taking a back-to-basics approach and using only natural ingredients. Instead of cutting corners, we take the time to make our yogurt using an authentic recipe.

Integrity

We strongly believe that access to affordable, nutritious, delicious yogurt made with only natural ingredients is a right, not a privilege, and we believe every food maker has a responsibility to provide people with better options.

Leadership

In less than 10 years, Chobani has become the No. 1-selling Greek Yogurt brand in America. Now, we offer 250+ products across numerous platforms—and we’re redefining what a food-focused health and wellness company looks like today.

Innovation

We believe that innovation goes beyond the cups we make. This is why we launched our Chobani™ Food Incubator, a first-of-its-kind program to invest in and cultivate emerging food entrepreneurs focused on making natural and affordable food.

Giving back

Since our earliest days, we've proudly supported our hometowns in New York and Idaho. We built a state-of-the-art youth baseball stadium and a community center, and continue to invest in our communities.

Our people

Our greatest impact is the one that we have on each other. We owe all of our successes to the wonderful people that contribute to our culture of compassion.
